About Fretistan Ltd

Fretistan Ltd is a professional construction company based in Kenya, specializing in the design and construction of buildings, roads, bridges, and water infrastructure. Our mission is to build your vision — from concept to completion — with excellence, precision, and integrity.

What We Do

  • 🏗️ Professional building construction (residential, commercial, and industrial)
  • 🛣️ Roads, bridges, and infrastructure construction
  • 🏢 Renovation, remodeling, and structural design services
  • 🔧 Civil engineering and comprehensive project management
  • 💧 Water drilling, borehole development, and water supply solutions

Our Vision & Values

We aim to become Kenya’s most trusted name in construction by maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and client satisfaction. Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to innovation, teamwork, and sustainability.
